Gabriel Fonseca

I am Gabriel Fonseca. I am an undergraduate majoring in Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley. My current interest is in rocket propulsion, particularly bi-propellant liquid engines. I enjoy putting my knowledge of Math and Physics to use through running and programing dynamics simulations and through getting hands on with manufacturing and machining parts.

I have club experience with overall rocket design, in avionics, propulsion and simulations. I also have led the organization of various career fairs and other events geared towards aerospace enthusiasts.
